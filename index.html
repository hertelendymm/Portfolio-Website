My hobbies are playing boardgames, Bridge and video games. My favourite video game is “I Expect You to Die”. It is a spy themed escape-room puzzle VR game. I play it on my Meta Quest 2. I lived and worked in Ireland. I own a Bonsai, that somehow still alive. I studied and lived in a boarding school during my High School years.
